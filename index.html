How to play

You play with 2 â€“ 10 people. There are few choices for truth or dare. The choices are: Kindergarden version, Classic version, Dirty version or Boyfriend/Girlfriend version. You can also choose between Tic Tac Toe, Crocodile Bite or Choose a Number. Whoever loses must do a truth or dare. At the Tic Tac Toe you go 1 against 1 until someone loses. With the Crocodile Bite you have to be lucky not to pick the bad tooth or you lose. You can play this game with up to 10 people. At Choose a Number you also have to be lucky not to choose the chosen number otherwise you lose. You can play this game with up to 10 people.

Rules

  • If you are under 18 years old you are not allowed to play the Dirty version.
  • If you are under 13 years old you are not allowed to play the Dirty version, Classic version and Boyfriend/Girlfriend version. You are only allowed to play kindergarden version.
  • You are not allowed to play alone. You play with 2 people or more. Maximum 10 people.
